About

I am a Senior Applied Research Manager and Principal Researcher at Apple, where I lead research initiatives in robotics, edge AI, and 3D computer vision. My work focuses on LLM post-training and optimization, advancing the state-of-the-art in autonomous systems and intelligent robotics.

I received dual Ph.D. degrees: one in Electrical Engineering from the City College of New York, City University of New York under the supervision of Prof. Jizhong Xiao at the CCNY Robotics Lab, and another in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I conducted my research at the State Key Lab of Robotics.

Research Interests: Robotics, Edge AI, LLM Post-training and Optimization, 3D Computer Vision
