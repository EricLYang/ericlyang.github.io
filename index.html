About

I am a Senior Research Manager and Principal Researcher at Apple, where I lead research initiatives in robotics, edge AI, and 3D computer vision. My work focuses on LLM post-training and optimization, advancing the state-of-the-art in autonomous systems and intelligent robotics.

I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the City College of New York, City University of New York under the supervision of Dr. Jizhong Xiao at the CCNY Robotics Lab. I also completed my master-doc research at the State Key Lab of Robotics, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

Research Interests: Robotics, Edge AI, LLM Post-training and Optimization, 3D Computer Vision
